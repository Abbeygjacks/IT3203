Key Concepts in Sustainable Farming

Understanding the principles behind our farming practices is essential to appreciating our commitment to the land and community.

1. Rotational Grazing

**Rotational Grazing** involves moving livestock systematically from one pasture section to another. This prevents overgrazing, allowing grass to fully recover, which improves soil health and carbon sequestration.

2. Soil Health

We prioritize **Soil Health** by minimizing tillage, using cover crops, and adding natural compost. Healthy soil retains water better, requires fewer external inputs, and produces more nutrient-dense food.

3. Farm-to-Table Model

Our **Farm-to-Table Model** means our products go directly from our fields to your home. This ensures maximum freshness and minimizes the environmental impact associated with long-distance shipping.
