Our Story

Welcome to Chase 'n Grace Farm! Our names are Kevin and Amy Jackson, and we are the proud owners and founders of this small family farm. Our story begins in southern West Virginia, where we both grew up with a love for farming and living a rural and self-sufficient lifestyle. After getting married at only 18 and 20 years old, we moved to Georgia to pursue careers in IT and real estate, but our passion for farming never faded. Fourteen years later, we started a family â€” we were blessed with a son and daughter. After years of working in a technology world and starting a family, it became apparent that we wanted a simpler life and to share how we grew up with our children. In the years that followed, we purchased several properties and worked to start small homesteads. Finally in 2022, we purchased nearly 50 acres in a small town in northwest Georgia called Chatsworth. Named after our kids, we are currently building our dream at Chase 'n Grace Farm.

About Me

Hello! My name is Abbagail Jackson. I am a sophomore at Kennesaw State University and majoring in IT. Also, I am a student in IT3203: Introduction to Web Development at KSU and this website is a class project for that course. My goal for this project was to create a chic, functional website that effectively illustrates my family's farm story, products, and values. This website is a reflection of my parents' and my love and hobby for farming. A few other of my hobbies include photography, drawing, and tennis, which I am on the KSU club tennis team.
